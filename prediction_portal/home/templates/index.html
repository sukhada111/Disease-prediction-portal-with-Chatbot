Mental wellness plays a role

Mental health plays a major role in people’s ability to maintain a good physical health. Mental illness, such as depression and anxiety, affect people’s ability to participate in health-promoting behaviours. Furthermore, the advent of pandemic has further worsened the mental-health conditions of the people worldwide. We aim at providing health-related assistance and solving mental-health issues using an AI Chatbot through which the patients can converse openly and get health related guidance.
